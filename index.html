Jack Frost

About Jack Frost

Jackson Overland Frost, better known as Jack Frost, is the main protagonist and narrator in Rise of the Guardians from a movie and an immortal supernatural being much like the Guardians. Unlike the others, however, he’s a loner, the classic rebel without a cause, sarcastic and mischievous. As the manifestation of winter, Jack Frost is capable of manipulating ice and snow. He is the spirit of mischief and chaos personified, but when he discovers the purpose behind his powers, he will become a true Guardian, representing "Fun."

Jack Frost and Rise of the Guardians

Jack Frost’s characteristics

  1. Jack Frost has white hair, pale skin, and bright blue eyes. He wears a blue hoodie, brown pants, and carries a wooden staff that he uses to control his powers.
  2. Jack is known for his mischievous and playful personality. He enjoys playing pranks on people and is often seen as a troublemaker. However, he also has a kind heart and cares deeply for children.
  3. Jack is immortal and has the ability to manipulate ice and snow. He can create blizzards, freeze objects, and even fly by riding on gusts of wind.
  4. Despite his carefree nature, Jack is also brave and selfless. He is willing to risk his own safety to protect others, especially children.
