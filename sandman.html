About Snadman

Sanderson Mansnoozie, or Sandman (Sandy), is the Guardian of Dreams and the first Guardian chosen by the Man in the Moon in Rise of the Guardians from a movie the Guardian of Dreams and the first Guardian chosen by the Man in the Moon in Rise of the Guardians. He is a small, golden, mute character who creates good dreams for children using Dreamsand. As the most powerful guardian, he represents peaceful sleep and is feared by Pitch Black.

Sandy

Sandman’s characteristics

  1. Manipulates yellow Dreamsand to form objects, weapons, or flying vehicles and can turn nightmares back into dreams.
  2. Communicates exclusively through silent, glowing imagery floating above his head.
  3. He was the first to realize Pitch Black's return, and he sacrificed himself in a battle against Pitch's nightmares before being resurrected by the children's belief.
  4. Gentle, kind, and artistic, but a fierce fighter when necessary.
  5. A short, stout, golden-colored being with sand-like hair styled into points.

Sandman’s friends

  • Jack Frost
  • Santa Claus
  • Tooth Fairy
  • Easter Bunny
